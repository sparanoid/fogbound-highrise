Sparanoid is a personal website about (web) designs, freehand drawings and interesting gadgets created by Tunghsiao Liu since 2005.

Tunghsiao Liu, a.k.a. Tongxiao Liu, is a (web) designer, amateur artist living in China, now study at Tianjin Academy of Fine Arts. Feel free to contact me via my email.

This site is NSFW.
